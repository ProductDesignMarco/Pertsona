Background & Role

Jordan has 7+ years of experience in environmental and financial analysis, with a Master's degree in Sustainability Management. At GreenEdge Capital, Jordan is responsible for integrating ESG factors into investment research, conducting materiality assessments, and tracking regulatory risks across global markets. Working closely with portfolio managers and compliance teams, Jordan helps guide ESG strategy in alignment with evolving global standards.

"My role is about making sustainability measurable. Data-driven ESG analysis isn’t just good ethics—it's critical to long-term investment performance and regulatory alignment."

Goals & Challenges

Primary Goals

  • Improve ESG scoring models for better investment decisions.
  • Ensure compliance with global disclosure frameworks like SFDR, CSRD, and TCFD.
  • Develop repeatable methodologies for ESG risk reporting.
  • Drive internal stakeholder education on ESG relevance.
  • Integrate third-party ESG data with internal financial systems.

Key Challenges

  • Fragmented data sources and inconsistent ESG metrics.
  • Difficulty quantifying environmental and social impacts.
  • Pressure to meet investor expectations with limited tools.
  • Keeping pace with shifting regulatory landscapes.
  • Cross-departmental misalignment on ESG priorities.
